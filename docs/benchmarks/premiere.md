# Adobe Premiere Benchmark

## Analysis Summary
Adobe Premiere is the industry standard for professional video editing.

## Key Features
- **Multi-Cam Support:** Robust tools for complex editing.
- **Dynamic Link:** Integration with After Effects and Photoshop.
- **Precision Trimming:** Professional-grade timeline tools.

## UI Style
- **Color Palette:** Dark-gray theme with professional blue/white highlights.
- **Layout:** Fully customizable panel system.
- **Complexity:** Highly detailed and multi-layered UI.

## CYBO Comparison
- **Strengths:** Precision and depth of tools.
- **Opportunity:** Re-imagining the video editor with AI-first workflows and refined web-based UI.
